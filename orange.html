Citrus sinensis

Contains:

a-pinene, sabinene, myrcene, limonene, linalool, citronellal, neral, geranial

Properties:

antiseptic, antidepressant, antispasmodic, anti-inflammatory, carminative, diuretic, cholagogue, sedative, tonic

Uses:

Diffused;

Mood boosting, stress.

Topically;

Reduces inflammation, brightens skin.

Cautions:

Some individuals may experience mild contact burns, treat as stated below.

Oil is phototoxic, avoid exposure to direct sunlight for 12hrs after topical application.

First Aid:

If skin irritation has occurred apply cornstarch or similar to absorb excess.

If gotten in the eye rinse with clear water for 15 min, if irritation persists for more than 30 min seek professional help.
