If you have a medical condition that requires regular use of pharmaceutical medication please also read the drug interactions section.

Hormonal conditions, blood sugar related conditions, bleeding disorders, and blood pressure are typically the most likely to experience bad effects from essential oils that also act on these systems.

Seizure conditions can also be affected by certain oils, please refer to that section within the app.

Other conditions may also be affected however at this time these are the known categories.

Hormones:

Negative reactions will usually have to do with birth control, cross sex hormones, and/or hormonally related cancers. Oils that influence hormones will bring things back to a biological balance which may be desired with hormone related cancers however there is a risk it could make things worse. When it comes to birth control and cross sex hormones it may reduce the effectiveness of these medications or interact in other ways.

Blood thinning:

These oils should be avoided for up to 2 weeks prior to surgery. Tragic results can occur.

  2. Garlic - Allium sativum
