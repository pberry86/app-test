Melaleuca viridiflora

Contains:

1,8-CINEOLE, Y-TERPINEOL, LIMONENE, A-PHELLANDRENE, A-PINENE, B-PINENE, LINALOOL, PIPERITONE

Properties:

Analgesic, anti-rheumatic, antiseptic, bactericidal, balsamic, cicatrisant, decongestant, expectorant, febrifuge, insecticide, stimulant, vermifuge, vulnerary

Uses:

Diffused;

Concentration, lung infections, pain relief, bronchitis, asthma, sinusitis, sore throats, catarrh, laryngitis

Topically;

Ulcers, acne, blemishes, boils, burns, cuts, insect bites, minor infections, digestion, pain relief

Cautions:

No specific cautions

First aid:

If skin irritation has occurred apply cornstarch or similar to absorb excess.

If gotten in the eye rinse with clear water for 15 min, if irritation persists for more than 30 min seek professional help.
