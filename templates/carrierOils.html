Olive oil - Natural antioxidant and exfoliant, deeply nourishing for the skin. Cheaper than coconut oil. High in Vit A and E.

Coconut oil - More moisturizing than olive oil, disinfectant and antimicrobial properties. High in proteins essential for proper skin renewal. Doesn’t go rancid.

Jojoba oil - Technically a liquid wax, plant is native to North America. Non-greasy texture, light weight and anti-inflammatory properties, doesn’t clog pores. Molecular structure very similar to the skin’s natural oils. Also doesn't go rancid.

Almond oil - Protects against and reverses UV damage. Evens out skin tone and can reduce scarring, reduces inflammation in the skin. A patch test should be done before using for the first time.
