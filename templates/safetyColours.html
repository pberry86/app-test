The names of each oil will be in one of the following colours according to a very basic overall safety profile. These colours cannot possibly account for individual health issues so please read each QRDS carefully.

All safety profiles are based off of unadulterated (pure) essential oils.

Green = All ages.

Purple = Safe for young children but NOT pregnant individuals.

Orange = Safe for pregnant individuals but NOT young children.

Red = Adults ONLY.

Black = Do not use. Only included for educational purposes.
