It is not recommended to use essential oils with babies under 6 months of age. For babies under 6 months of age please see the section on hydrosols.

The first thing that needs to be understood is the concentration level of essential oils. Imagine you have a shot of espresso, now fill a large mug with espresso shots, now condense that mugful into a fraction of the original single shot. For instance it takes 60,000 roses to produce 1 ounce of pure rose oil.

The second, is to remember that the smaller the body the faster a high concentration is reached. For example proper dosing of pharmaceutical medications is done based on body weight. The same principle applies with any form of natural medicine.

And the third, is stages of neurological development. Earlier stages are more delicate than later stages.
